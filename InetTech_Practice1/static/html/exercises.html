English grammar exercises

Translate sentences into English:

  1. Task: Вони обідають о 12 годині

    Answer: They have lunch at 12 o'clock

  2. Task: Ти любиш плавати?

    Answer: Do you like to swim?

  3. Task: Я зараз дивлюся телевізор

    Answer: I am watching TV now

Choose if statements are true or false:

  1. Task: "To read" is a regular verb.

    Answer: False

  2. Task: "To drive" is an irregular verb.

    Answer: True
